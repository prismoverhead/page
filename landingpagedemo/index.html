FAQ

Will this work with my existing phone number?

Yes. We typically forward your existing business number to the AI agent, or connect it through a VoIP provider. Your callers dial the same number they already know.

Can I still answer calls myself?

Yes. You can choose rules: AI answers all calls, after-hours only, or only when you don’t pick up in a few rings. You stay in control.

What tools does this integrate with?

Out of the box, it works with Google Sheets and Google Calendar. We can discuss additional integrations on Pro and Premium plans.

How long does setup take?

Most companies are live in under a week: we configure your script, scheduling rules, and connect your tools.

Is my customer data secure?

Yes. We use secure, encrypted connections and follow best practices for storing and accessing call and job data.
